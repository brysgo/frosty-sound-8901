The Complete AI Stack for Solo Accountants and Bookkeepers

Introduction

If you run a solo accounting or bookkeeping practice, your days are shaped by a specific kind of cognitive load: context-switching between clients, chasing documents, writing the same explanatory emails over and over, and trying to stay current on software that changes quarterly. You’re not managing a team—you’re managing everything, including your own attention.

AI has been noisy for three years. Most of the hype has been irrelevant to your work. But something shifted in 2024 and 2025: the tools got specific enough to be genuinely useful in this vertical. Not “AI will automate accounting”—that’s still nonsense—but AI that handles the surrounding work: communication, research, document review, report drafting. The repetitive cognitive work that fills the gaps between billable hours.

The opportunity isn’t to replace your expertise. It’s to stop doing things that don’t require it.
